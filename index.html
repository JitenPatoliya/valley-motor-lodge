About Us

Valley Motor Lodge is nestled in the heart of the Minnedosa.

Located just 2 hours west of Winnipeg and 30 minutes north of Brandon, Minnedosa is a wonderful get a way for the whole family.

Enjoy the fabulous beach area, visit the Heritage Village or experience the excellent hunting or fishing then head to the Valley Motor Lodge to relax and recharge.

  • Minnedosa Golf And Country Club.
  • Museum & Heritage Village.
  • River Edge's Recreation Park.

Attractions

Explore all that Minnedosa has to offer. More info Discover Minnedosa

Golfing

18 holes of beautiful valley surroundings, the course offers elevation changes of 120 feet and is home to a wonderful friendly clubhouse with pro shop, licensed lounge and snack shop. Contact minngolf@mts.net or call 204-867-3151.

Room Types: Triple, Double & Single

Hunting

Located along the central flyway Minnedosa is home to a wide variety of ducks and geese. See Dirty Lake Outfitters for more information.

Hunters are welcome to use the room provided and freezers at no additional charge. Pets are welcome.

Lake & Beach Area

There are so many ways to enjoy the lake. Summer time check out the beautiful sandy beaches, swimming, picnic areas, playgrounds, concession, boat launch, fishing, tubing or water skiing. Winter you will find - pond hockey (www.skatethelake.ca), Winterfest Celebration of winter and family, cross country skiers, snowmobilers including a staging area or ice fishing.

Room Types

All rooms have been recently renovated. Rooms include fridge, microwave, coffee maker, full cable and wireless internet access.

Triple - 2 double beds + 1 queen. There are 2 triples available that can be joining suites.

Double - 2 double beds.

Single - 1 queen bed + futon.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations for individual room reservations are permitted 24 hours prior on weekdays and 48 hours prior on weekends.

Applies to block bookings only
Please read the following information on our reservation and cancellation policies.

  • All rooms reserved must be confirmed at least 6 weeks in advance of booking date. All rooms must be confirmed by a name and credit card number. Any rooms not confirmed will be classified as available to other customers.
  • All reservations are subject to a 7 day cancellation period. Any room not cancelled within 7 days of date booked will be charged. NO EXCEPTIONS. Any multiple night bookings will be charged the total nights reserved regardless of early check-out.
  • All rooms are newly-renovated and non-smoking. All include coffee-maker, microwave, and fridge. We also have full cable TV, free wireless internet, and use of the hospitality lounge.
